Why I Admire Him

Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India. He played a vital role in India's space and missile development programs and inspired millions of students through his vision and humility.

Despite achieving great success, he lived a simple life and believed strongly in education, hard work, and dreaming big.
